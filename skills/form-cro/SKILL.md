# Form CRO

This skill is the **form-cro** workflow from the [skills.sh leaderboard #359](https://skills.sh/coreyhaines31/marketingskills/form-cro) (procedural content aligned with [coreyhaines31/marketingskills `cro` → `references/form.md`](https://github.com/coreyhaines31/marketingskills/tree/main/skills/cro); upstream v2 merged `form-cro` into `cro`, but the skills.sh catalog retains this focused entry). Maximize form completion rates while capturing only essential data.

## When to use

- Lead capture, contact, demo, application, survey, checkout, or quote forms
- Form abandonment, low completion rate, or field-level drop-off
- Multi-step forms, validation, error messaging, or mobile form UX

**Not for:**

- **`skills/page-cro/SKILL.md`** — homepage, landing, pricing, or feature page conversion (page-level CRO)
- **`skills/onboarding-cro/SKILL.md`** — post-signup activation and first-run product onboarding
- Signup/registration account-creation flows (`skills/signup-flow-cro/SKILL.md`)

## Procedure

Read and follow **`skills/form-cro/references/form.md`** for the full form optimization framework: initial assessment, field-by-field guidance, multi-step patterns, error handling, trust elements, form-type playbooks, mobile optimization, measurement, and experiment ideas.

## Output format

Deliver findings as:

1. **Form audit** — issues with impact, fix, and priority
2. **Recommended form design** — required/optional fields, order, copy, errors, layout
3. **Test hypotheses** — A/B ideas with expected outcomes

Pair with **`skills/analytics-tracking/SKILL.md`** for event tracking when measuring form completion and field drop-off.
