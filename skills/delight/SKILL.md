# Delight (personality and memorable moments)

This skill is the **delight** workflow from the [skills.sh leaderboard](https://skills.sh/pbakaus/impeccable/delight) (sourced from [pbakaus/impeccable](https://github.com/pbakaus/impeccable)). It finds natural moments where small surprises, voice, motion, or craft turn a functional interface into one people remember—without delaying work, hiding weak UX, or defaulting to gimmicks.

## Mandatory preparation

Before adding delight:

1. Read **`skills/frontend-design/SKILL.md`** for tone-appropriate craft and visual direction.
2. Read **`skills/web-design-guidelines/SKILL.md`** for motion, accessibility (`prefers-reduced-motion`), and interaction guardrails.
3. Read **`skills/impeccable/SKILL.md`** for register (brand vs product), context gathering, and anti-pattern lists.

If brand personality, audience, or how playful the surface should be is unclear from the codebase, ask the user directly before shipping jokes, sound, or celebration UI.

## Procedure

Follow **`reference/delight.md`** end to end for register-specific opportunities, techniques, verification, and polish handoff.

When moments feel earned and scoped, continue with **`skills/polish/SKILL.md`** for the final ship pass.
